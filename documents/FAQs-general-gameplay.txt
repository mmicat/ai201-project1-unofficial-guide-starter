Extra
What counts towards the Small Goal, Make a Billion course?
DOES count:

    Blings collected on the floor/roof/etc around the normal map
    Blings collected on caverns
    Blings dropped by Greedy Pouches
    Picking blings up while on a whimcycle


DOES NOT count:

    Blings for monster kills (location doesn't matter)
    Blings collected on dungeons (Dream Warehouse, Well of Fortune, etc)
    Blings rewarded for mini-games
    After hitting daily cap, every bling stops counting


What this means for people that want to work towards this goal:

    Following a bling route as soon as you start playing for the day will mean you have way less than the 160k cap being counted towards the goal.
    Since monster kills and dungeon blings count towards the daily limit, leave those for later in the day, to maximize the amount of bling you collect daily that is valid.
    If the course is showing all the correct 0s and you actually need 10mil and not 1billion for this, and if you somehow only collect bling that counts until hitting the limit, it'll take a minimum of 63 days to get this goal.


What this means for normal people:

    Chill, it's gonna take a while.

COMMAND: -dyeoff
CATEGORY: General Gameplay
DESCRIPTION: How to disable dye on an outfit piece
CONTENT: How do I disable dye on an outfit piece?How do I turn off dye?
There are two ways to disable dye on an outfit piece.
Method 1: Turn Off Dye
PC/Mobile: In the bottom right-hand corner of the selected piece, there will be a rainbow paint palette. If there is a white background around it, the dye is on. Click it to turn it off. You can turn it back on by clicking it again.
PS5: Press Square to open the dye slot selection on an outfit piece (do not long press as this will open Dye Mode instead). Next, press X to unselect the dye slot, then press O twice to back out and click Save.
Method 2: Remove Dye
Select an outfit piece and open up the Dye menu. Click "Select All", then choose a random dye. Click the same dye again and then Save. This will save the original colours as a dyeing scheme.

COMMAND: -eureka
CATEGORY: General Gameplay
DESCRIPTION: How to get eurekas / how to change the color
CONTENT: How can I get more Eurekas?
You can get more Eurekas by spending Vital Energy in the Realm of Eureka.
Please see this sheet for max stats and labels.
How can I change my Eureka's color?
You can change each Eureka's color by upgrading it to level 10, giving you a random color (which may be the original color). If you want a specific color, you may have to level up multiple Eurekas.
Because 3-star Eureka's can only be upgraded to level 5, they cannot change colors.
How do I claim the Eureka rewards in the Compendium?
To be considered complete, you must have all 3 eurekas from a set in the specified color. For example, to claim the reward for First Snow: White color, you must own the upgraded First Snow: Head, First Snow: Hands and First Snow: Feet in white.

    The base color does not count at Level 0 - you must level the Eureka to level 10.


You can locate the Eureka Compendium in Outfit Compendium > Exploration Season > Radiant Whim > Eureka Collection

COMMAND: -evolution
CATEGORY: General Gameplay
DESCRIPTION: How set evolution works
CONTENT: How do I evolve an outfit? What do evolutions do?
To evolve an outfit, you will need to obtain the outfit twice or get Heartshines. For crafted outfits, you must craft the outfit again. Outfits from limited or permanent banners will instead require pulling the outfit again. 

5-star outfits will only need one additional copy used for the final evolution. The first two evolutions will use Heartshines obtained from Deep Echo milestones or from Advanced Courses. Evolving an outfit can unlock new colors or patterns for an outfit, which is purely cosmetic and does not change stats.

How do I get more Calm/Heartfelt Thoughts to evolve sets?
You will need to continue through the story until the end of chapter 7 and defeat the boss to unlock the Realm of Breakthrough, where you can farm the materials once a week.

COMMAND: -friends
CATEGORY: General Gameplay
DESCRIPTION: How to add friends
CONTENT: How can I add friends?
After progressing through the story enough to unlock Pear-Pal, you can access the Friends menu. Once in the menu, click the "Add Friends" button, then either enter your friend's code in the top bar or generate your own friend code by clicking "Get Friend Code" at the bottom.

    Friend Codes will expire after 30 days.
    You can only add friends who are on the same server as you
        An America server player can add another America server player
        An America server player cannot add a Europe server player
    Friend Codes for each server are case-sensitive, 8-characters, and all start with a different character
        Asia = f ; HK/TW/Macau = K ; Europe = 2 ; America = n
    Player IDs cannot be used to add others


If you would like to add other players, visit your server's dedicated code sharing thread in its related forum in the CO-OP category!

COMMAND: -glowup

CONTENT: How do I unlock the Glow Up feature?
Wishfield pieces: Continue through the main Wishfield story until you've reached Chapter 3, then visit the app on your Pear-Pal!
Itzaland pieces: Continue through the main Itzaland story until you complete Chapter 2, then you will get a Pear-Pal message from Davie Lopp. Go see him near the Shroomville warp spire and complete his quest "New Training : Warp Spire Realm".

Every item can be leveled to 10 using Shiny Bubbles. You will then be able to upgrade one final time ("glow up") using Glitter Bubbles (Wishfield pieces) or  Swirl Bubbles (Itzaland pieces). Both are obtained from their respective Realm of Dark levels or Recycling!

Please note that only level 11 (max) items are considered "glowed up".

CONTENT: General Resonance Banner Rules:

    You are guaranteed to get every item in a set without duplicates.
    You can keep track of pity using the official Whim-log. You can also see the history of a currently running Resonance.
    You can use Revelation Crystals :zRevelationCrystal_in: for both 5-Star Limited Resonance and 4-Star Limited Resonance. You can convert diamonds :zDiamond_in:  to :zRevelationCrystal_in: in the Store.
    You can only use Epiphany Crystals :zEpiphanyCrystal_in: on 4-Star Resonance Banners. These are only available via real money purchase.
    You can only use Resonite Crystals:zResoniteCrystal_in: on the Permanent Resonance Banner.


5-Star Limited Resonance Pity Rules: 

    The maximum number of pulls required to complete a 5-star set is 20 * the number of pieces in the set.
    For example, A 10-piece set will take a maximum of 200 pulls to complete.
    Every 10 pulls, you are guaranteed a 4-star item or higher.
    Every 20 pulls, you are guaranteed a 5-star item.
    If your 4-star item and 5-star item are to hit on the same pull (example, if both are expected on pull #20) the 5-star takes priority and the 4-star item will appear on the next pull.
    For the final evolution on a 5-star set, you must pull the entire outfit again.
    To unlock the evolution on the 4-star set on the connected banner, you must pull the entire 4-star set again.
    You can select one 5-star item Ocean's Blessing which guarantees that 5-star item within your first 5-star items after selecting the Blessing.
    You cannot select a new Ocean's Blessing during the same patch, so choose carefully.
    Ocean's Blessings progress does not carry over and will be reset on the rerun.


5-Star Permanent Resonance Pity Rules:

    When selecting a set through Tidal Resonance,  you will only receive 5-star items from that set until it is completed, or if you changed the target set.
    Tidal Guidance attempts are reset when you select "Give Up" and these attempts are not refunded.


4-Star Resonance Pity Rules:

    The maximum number of pulls required to complete a 4-star set is 5 * the number of pieces in the set.
    Every 5 pulls, you are guaranteed a 4-star item.
    To unlock the evolution, you must pull the entire set twice.

CONTENT: Do the nodes in the Heart of Infinity work for Mira Crown?
Yes, they do. There are three sets of nodes that improve your overall score:

    The Wishfield percentage nodes (blue), split into Pre-Interlude and Post-Interlude sections, increase your clothing stats by up to 12% and your clothing score by up to 8% respectively.
    The Wishfield flat nodes (yellow) are split into 10 nodes per Style (50 total) and increase all clothing stats by up to +100 per Style node.
        Note: all flat nodes will increase your clothing stats, even if the style doesn't correspond to the challenge, e.g., Sweet flat nodes will still help in a Cool challenge.
    The Sea of Stars percentage nodes (pink) increase all score (clothing and eureka) by up to 10% total.

These all increase your final score.
How do I unlock these nodes?

    The Pre-Interlude blue nodes are available as soon as you start the game.
    The Post-Interlude blue nodes are unlocked after completing the Interlude chapter, Call of Beginnings.
    The yellow flat nodes require unlocking all of the Silvergale's Aria pieces and makeup sketch nodes first.
    The Sea of Stars pink nodes can be gradually unlocked by acquiring the Sea of Stars sketch and Heartshine Shard nodes in the Shard: Sea of Stars. These nodes are unlocked with Ethereal Stars, obtained through Starlit Moments daily tasks.

COMMAND: -sos
CATEGORY: General Gameplay
DESCRIPTION: What happens when you have enough Ethereal Stars to complete the Shard: Sea of Stars
CONTENT: Why am I not getting my Ethereal Stars?
Once you have enough Ethereal Stars to unlock everything in the Shard: Sea Of Stars, any extra stars get converted into 2 Starlit Crystals each. This brings your total for Starlit Moments dailies from 54 Starlit Crystals daily to 76.

Please note that not everything needs to be unlocked, you just need enough Ethereal Stars gathered.
Currencies in the Sea of Stars
All of these may be collected once a day after holding the bell near a warp point.

    Starlit Shard
        100 per day
        Automatically converted to Starlit Crystals at a 25:1 ratio
    Starlit Crystal
        54 per day (76 when HoI shard is completed); 30 from gathering in map, 20 from dailies, 4 from shard conversion
        Used to exchange in the Starlit Shop
        When the Starlit Pursuit event is active, you may obtain 120 extra Crystals per week
    Ethereal Star 
        11 per day; 3 from gathering, 8 from dailies
        Used in the Heart of Infinity - Shard: Sea of Stars
        Converts to Starlit Crystals at a 1:2 ratio once you have collected enough to complete the Shard

DESCRIPTION: How to access the Choo-Choo Train
CONTENT: Where do I go for the Choo-Choo Train rides?
You will need to have completed the Home on the Rails quest first. The Choo-Choo Train has a set pattern it goes through around the Abandoned District. Please see the map below for stations. 

It takes about 3 minutes in-between each station and the entire loop takes 22 minutes (real time). This loop is not based on in-game time and will keep running as long as the game is open.

To quickly catch the train, restart your game at any station!

COMMAND: -vivian
CATEGORY: General Gameplay
DESCRIPTION: Where to find Vivian
CONTENT: How to find Vivian?
In order to start looking for Vivian you must first challenge all other members from the Extreme Stylist Group (Brent, Testa, Elio and Reibel). After defeating them, a yellow circle will appear on the map to the southwest of the coliseum. Go there and investigate Vivan's Research Notes. After checking her notes, open Pear-Pal > Factions > Extreme Stylist Group > Vivian and click 'Deduct'. 

Select:

    near the water source and campfire
    the Titan's Graveyard


When the Deduction is complete, the quest 'Leader Showdown! Risk-Taking Adventurer' will appear. Go to the campsite in the Titan's Graveyard that the tracker shows and interact first with Vivian's Experiment Records then with the shiny spot behind the wooden crates. Nikki will find a recipe to craft the Revealing Potion and you must use the ingredients in order:

    Pendulum Fruit
    Knotted Fern
    Revealing Potion


After following these steps Vivian will appear and you can challenge her.
