FAQ
Account Definitions

    What is an Infold account?
        It is an account created using an email
    What is a third party account?
        It is an account created using (PSN, Google, Apple, Facebook)
        You can turn a third party account into a Infold account by binding an email to the account
    What is an Epic account?
        It is an account used to log in into the Epic Games Store launcher and is considered a separate sign-in menu from the game
        


General Binding Questions

    Can I connect multiple third party accounts?
        Yes, and you can unbind any third party accounts by going Account Security > Security Binding > Change Binding
    How do I find the sign-in option?
        You can find it on the login screen and the Infold website
    Will I need to use a different email to make an Infold account if my third party account is already connected to that email?
        No, you can use the same email as Infold and third party accounts are seen as separate
    Can I use a different email than those I use for my third party account(s)?
        Yes, you can use any email you'd like
    If I want to use a different email for another Infold account will I be able to unbind it?
        Yes, review the guide (https://discord.com/channels/1245702850340978751/1349219578647154818)
    Can I bind using a phone number?
        No, this option is not available.


PSN Related Questions

    What is an PSN account?
        It is an account you use to sign in via Playstation (PS5)
    How do I find my PSN information?
        Your PSN sign-in information is the login you used to sign-in and create a account for the Playstation Network
            If you do not remember the login, try using the recovery methods on the PSN website


Customer Service/Infold Website Questions

    Why can't I access the customer service button?
        If the button is unavailable for any reason, you can use the online portal or contact Infold by email
    Why is there no prompt when I access customer service?
        You can access it by using the blue headphone button next to the chat box and switch to live-chat
            If you do not see the button, switch into desktop mode on mobile devices by changing it in the browser settings, or use the Google app in your device's Playstore/Appstore


Miscellaneous Account Linking Questions

    The survey says its already been submitted; would I still be able to get my reward?
        It should be sent to you as a separate mail when the form is completed, try re-opening the game and checking the mail tab
            If it persists, submit a bug report

Infold website (https://account.infoldgames.com/login)


If none of these links help, please contact support through the online portal!
For a guide on filling out a Bug Report, please see this thread.

Please note the Customer Service hours are 10:00 - 22:00 (UTC+8)

For concerns about uninstalling recent Windows updates as a potential workaround for the black/white screen issue:

    Uninstalling an update is not inherently dangerous. Microsoft provides the option to uninstall updates to resolve compatibility or stability issues.
    Security risks are low, and temporarily uninstalling updates does not create security issues unless the system is exposed to vulnerabilities not addressed by that specific update. Practicing safe browsing is always advised and the update can be reinstalled later when it is more stable or a new patch is released.

Running your game in compatibility mode can cause performance issues and increase your PC temperature - it is not recommended for long periods of use.

CONTENT: How do I change my keybindings on PC?
Only a few keys may be rebound at this time. To do so, follow the steps below: 

    Go to the Pear-Pal
    Click Settings
    Click Controls on the left side bar
    Under the controls section find Key Settings
    Click Go
    Click any of the buttons and enter any key

Note: As of currently, grey color buttons are unable to be changed 

Can I change my keybindings/controller mappings on Mobile/PS5? 
You currently cannot change your keybindings on any other device. There is no news on changing keybindings or controller remapping for other devices at this time. 

You can submit feedback regarding this issue through their Optimization Suggestions and Feedback Submission Form.

COMMAND: -uid
CATEGORY: Misc and Customer Support
DESCRIPTION: Explains how to find your in-game ID/UID/Stylist ID
CONTENT: How do I find my in-game ID/UID/Stylist ID?
You may find your in-game ID, known as your UID or Stylist ID, on the right side of your screen near your avatar when opening Pearpal. This number will also be shown on the bottom left of your screen while playing. 

How do I find my ID if I can't access the game?
You can login on the Infold Website with your respective sign-in option and find your UID under Account Info > My Games > Infinity Nikki.
  

    Alternatively, you can also check by logging into the Official Interactive Map, Whim-Log or the Top-Up Website.


Additionaly, if you have a friend who is able to open your profile, your ID will be listed as the "Stylist ID". 

PC players may also find their UID (as a folder name) when going to this file path:
C:\Program Files\InfinityNikkiGlobal Launcher\InfinityNikkiGlobal\X6Game\Saved\GamePlayPhotos
This path may be slightly different for players who use the Epic launcher or Steam.
