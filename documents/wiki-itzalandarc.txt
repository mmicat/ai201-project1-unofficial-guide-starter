ITZALAND PROLOGUE: WHERE THE DIVINITY LIES
===================================================================
Quest Type: Main Quest
Position: The tenth chapter of the Main Questline and the first chapter of the Itzaland arc.
Previous Chapter: End (Wishfield Chapter)
Next Chapter: Itzaland Chapter 1: A Place Blessed by the Mothershroom

Quests:
- Where the Divinity Lies

Total Rewards:
- Mira EXP: 500
- Diamond: 50
- Bling: 12K
- Thread of Purity: 50
- Shiny Bubbles: 50

Synopsis:
Nikki and Momo travel through the Abandoned District and cross into the Itzaland Canyon. On their journey through the canyon, they encounter a landslide and an inexplicable sandstorm. They spot a mysterious light that gives Nikki the Purifying Wind outfit and leads them through a cave. Along the way, Nikki hears a song and someone speaking. Upon reaching the end of the cave, they come across a sealed exit. The light guides Nikki into breaking through the seal and stepping through it.

On the other side, Nikki and Momo find themselves in a golden space full of floating ruins. A voice calls to Nikki, pleading with her to fulfill their wish related to Echeros, and the two make their way through the space. After finding a chained draconic creature, a featureless woman made of light talks with Nikki and passes on her wish to resolve the problems plaguing this land's faith of Echeros. The Heart of Infinity resonates with this wish, allowing Nikki to take on the woman's fate within Itzaland.

Nikki awakes to find herself in the middle of a ceremony attended by Shroomlings and humans. Startled by this and Medael behind her, she slips and loses consciousness upon hitting the ground.

Characters: Momo, Nikki, Saintess

ITZALAND CHAPTER 1: A PLACE BLESSED BY THE MOTHERSHROOM
===================================================================
Quest Type: Main Quest
Position: The eleventh chapter in the Main Questline and the second chapter of the Itzaland arc.
Previous Chapter: Itzaland Prologue: Where the Divinity Lies
Next Chapter: Itzaland Chapter 2: Land of Falsehoods, Path to Truth

Quests:
- New Beginnings in Itzaland
- Beware of the Behemoth!
- The Shroom Crown's Witness
- Quest for Lost Memories

Total Rewards:
- Mira EXP: 2K
- Resonite Crystal: 2
- Diamond: 100
- Bling: 40K
- Thread of Purity: 200
- Shiny Bubbles: 200
- Rayplume: Head: 1
- Rayplume: Hands: 1
- Rayplume: Feet: 1
- Quirky Shroomling (Sketch)
- Shroomling Curiosity (Sketch)

Synopsis — Part 1: New Beginnings in Itzaland
Nikki wakes surrounded by Shroomlings and Momo explains that she's been asleep for three days. He also explains that the Shroomlings are worshipping her as their Saintess. Unsure of what to do, Nikki claims ignorance from her fall at the coming-of-age ceremony and learns that none of the Shroomlings have seen the Saintess's appearance before now. They decide to make use of this to assume the Saintess's identity to discover more about the god and the mysterious girl.

Nikki and Momo come across Luminana, who indirectly explains that the Shroomlings previously worshipped the Mothershroom but have had their worship supplanted by worship of the Titan King by the Docilists. Continuing to the Snail Ranch, they learn from Gulitott that the Docilists believe that the Titan King's name is Echeros. From the little Shroomlings, they learn that the Mothershroom is sick and that the Docilists have been preparing for a ceremony to heal the Mothershroom.

The little Shroomlings take Nikki and Momo to see Whirly, the Saintess's snail. Docilist attendants arrive to inform Nikki that Medael has summoned the Saintess back to Spira to prepare for the Healing of the Mothershroom ceremony. When Nikki and Momo hesitate to return, a Parksolian named Angert intervenes and makes an excuse for them to remain in the Snail Ranch for a while longer. Angert and the Docilists then depart. Nikki and Momo decide to visit the Mothershroom and are accompanied by the little Shroomlings.

Synopsis — Part 2: Beware of the Behemoth!
Nikki and Momo follow the little Shroomlings to Shroomville but find out that a Behemoth is loose in the settlement. They find Fulpapa, who was accidentally trapped behind crates by the Behemoth, and he explains that the Behemoth was likely attracted by the Thornfruit collected by his brother Fulwawa. Nikki crafts a Gigantification Ability Outfit and frees Fulpapa.

Nikki and Momo find Fulpapa trapped on a tree with the Behemoth at the tree base. The little Shroomlings explain that the Behemoth can be scared off with a roar. Using the Gigantification Ability, Nikki stuns the Behemoth with a roar. Fulwawa is able to leave the tree and Fulpapa arrives with the last Thornfruit. Nikki throws the Thornfruit away from Shroomville to lead the Behemoth out. With the danger averted, Nikki, Momo, and the Shroomlings continue their trek towards the Mothershroom woods.

Synopsis — Part 3: The Shroom Crown's Witness
Nikki and Momo arrive at the Mothershroom Woods. A forest keeper, Kabubu, shares that the Healing Ceremony is meant to remove the thing making the Mothershroom sick and that the Mothershroom rarely got sick before when the Guardian Beast was still around. Kabubu explains that the Guardian Beast is a huge snail that watches over the Mothershroom and that it's been missing for three years. The Mothershroom fell ill shortly after the Guardian Beast went missing and Medael had said that the illness was caused by the Shroomlings forgetting the true deity of the land, the Titan King. Kabubu also shares that Medael told the Shroomlings that the Guardian Beast is currently at Parkya Crater Lake.

Arriving at the Mothershroom, Nikki and Momo are pulled into the Mothershroom's dream. Navigating through the dream, the Mothershroom shows them a mechanical seed located inside that's draining its life energy. Unable to purify it inside of the dream, the Mothershroom brings them out of the dream. Nikki and Momo find themselves falling towards the ground but Domibaba spots them and is able to break their fall.

Synopsis — Part 4: Quest for Lost Memories
Nikki, Momo, and Domibaba introduce themselves to each other. Nikki blames her lack of knowledge on memory loss and Domibaba gives her a notebook to help sort out her thoughts. He also takes her and Momo to see the Fairy doctor, Lumira, to help recover the Saintess's memories.

Arriving at Lumira's cabin, they encounter and defeat a large group of Esselings that had been attracted by Lumira's experiment. When Lumira arrives, they explain Nikki's condition to her and she instructs Nikki to enter a meditative state so that the Moondew Bloom can help provide clues to Nikki's questions. Nikki learns that the mark given to her by the mysterious girl is a divine bond and that her past is entwined with a forgotten god and Itzaland, and that she will have a reunion with someone on her journey to heal the Mothershroom. Lumira gifts Nikki a Moonlit Stone that may help her recover lost memories.

Lumira's guidance directs Nikki's investigation towards Shell Island. Domibaba takes her and Momo to the dock where Bolgugu helps them cross the water to the island. Upon arrival, the three discuss where to go next and settle on visiting the Interim Stylist's Guild to ask about Docilism from a different perspective.

Characters: Angert, Buribaba, Dardada, Domibaba, Fulpapa, Fulwawa, Gulitott, Kabubu, Kuyelolo, Luminana, Lumira, Migugu, Momo, Nakaka, Nikki, Olinini, Rabubu

ITZALAND CHAPTER 2: LAND OF FALSEHOODS, PATH TO TRUTH
===================================================================
Quest Type: Main Quest
Position: The twelfth chapter in the Main Questline and the third chapter in the Itzaland arc.
Previous Chapter: Itzaland Chapter 1: A Place Blessed by the Mothershroom
Next Chapter: Itzaland Chapter 3: Fading Prayers, But in Whose Name?

Quests:
- Emerging Hidden Traces
- Whispers of Truth
- The Unveiling

Total Rewards:
- Mira EXP: 2.4K
- Resonite Crystal: 1
- Diamond: 100
- Bling: 15K
- Thread of Purity: 240
- Shiny Bubbles: 240
- Old Wings (Sketch)
- Consecutive Jumps (Sketch)

Synopsis — Part 1: Emerging Hidden Traces
Nikki and Momo enter the Interim Stylist's Guild and meet the president, Hilsetti. They probe for information about Docilism and he redirects them to the stylist Testa. After a Styling Challenge, Testa shares that the Guardian Beast isn't located at Parkya Crater Lake and suggests for them to talk to Ada. Nikki and Momo ask Ada about Docilism and she reveals that the Docilists have been isolating and ordering around the Shroomlings.

Returning to the entrance of the Interim Stylist's Guild, Nikki and Momo deduce that the Docilists have been manipulating the Shroomlings. Shortly after, they spot Docilists beginning a search for the Saintess. Domibaba arrives and explains that Medael has summoned the Saintess back to Spira to prepare for the Healing of the Mothershroom. He helps Nikki and Momo escape detection to continue their investigation. While escaping, Nikki is pulled away by an adventurer named Wilhelm. Wilhelm coerces Nikki into coming with her to the Coliseum. Nikki, Momo, and Wilhelm escape on the Sky Monarch Swanara.

Riding Swanara, the three make their way to the Coliseum.

Synopsis — Part 2: Whispers of Truth
Nikki, Momo, and Wilhelm learn from the Shroomling guards that there have been strange sounds coming from inside the Coliseum. Using Nikki's status as Saintess, they obtain permission to enter the Coliseum and open the doors using the Gigantification ability.

Inside the Coliseum, Wilhelm leaves Nikki and Momo to conduct her own investigation. The two make their way through the Coliseum and evade a Behemoth. They find a mural of the Titan King and a dragon where Nikki sees a vision. She learns that the Titan King imprisoned the dragon and called it a god, suggesting that the Titan King isn't the true god of Itzaland. Continuing through the Coliseum, Nikki and Momo reunite with Wilhelm before they're forced to escape the Behemoth chasing them. They find the empty pedestal that used to hold the Holy Spear.

After exiting the Coliseum, Nikki confronts Wilhelm about her intentions and confesses that she's been investigating Docilism. Wilhelm decides to trust Nikki and shares the information she found about a secret warehouse used by the Docilists. The three resolve to investigate the warehouse together and set off to rest at an adventurer camp.

Synopsis — Part 3: The Unveiling
At the adventurer camp, Domibaba finds Nikki, Momo, and Wilhelm. They clear up the misunderstanding about Wilhelm kidnapping Nikki and continue on to the camp. Wilhelm meets with Lee, deputy of the expedition team, and he gives the group his expedition notes to help them find the Docilist warehouse before he departs. The group rests for the night and sets up a group chat on Pear-Pal to keep in touch as they search for the warehouse. They name their group "Shroom Warriors."

The next day, Nikki and Momo use Lee's notes to find the warehouse in a cavern and reunite with Wilhelm and Domibaba. After clearing the entrance, they enter the cavern and eventually find snails trapped in cages, including Valor. They free the snails and discover that each snail has a mechanical seed on its shell. Nikki catches Domibaba up about her suspicions about the Docilist plot with the Mothershroom and he's shaken by the revelation. When Wilhelm arrives, Nikki explains everything to her as well.

Nikki purifies the snails of the mechanical seeds and the Shroom Warriors discover that the cavern is located right behind the Snail Ranch, which explains how snails had gone missing from the ranch. Nikki decides that she will return to Spira to expose the Docilists' plan and Wilhelm wishes her luck.

Characters: Ada, Domibaba, Hilsetti, Lee, Momo, Nikki, Pamimi, Patott, Testa, Wilhelm

Synopsis — Part 1: The Snail-Shelled Shroomling Land
With the Docilists requesting the Saintess to return to Spira, Nikki and Momo retrieve Whirly from the little Shroomlings at the Snail Ranch. After bidding farewell to the little Shroomlings, they ride Whirly up the giant spear and enter Spira. There, Nikki crafts the Shadow Swiftweave outfit to navigate through the city and reunites with Domibaba in front of the Docilism church. Domibaba reminisces about the past and his teacher and, with Nikki's encouragement, resolves to be a hero for her and the Shroomlings. Together, the three enter the Docilism church.

Inside, Medael interrogates Nikki about her whereabouts after leaving the Snail Ranch and her change in attitude. As Nikki fends off Medael's questions, the mark appears on Nikki's neck with a sear of pain and Medael dissipates the mark. With a warning to stay in line, Medael dismisses Nikki.

Outside, Domibaba explains that Medael and Docilist commander they met prior are Parksolians from the Boneyard and that Medael had been the one to bring Docilism to the Shroomlings. With the Shroomlings of Spira busy with preparations for the Healing of the Mothershroom, the Saintess is also required to participate in the Flower Offering Ceremony later in the day. Domibaba suggests to Nikki and Momo that the two return to the home of the Saintess to look for more clues until the time for the Flower Offering Ceremony arrives.

Synopsis — Part 2: Soft Prayers in the Wind
Upon returning to the home of the Saintess, Nikki and Momo encounter three little Shroomlings who ask her to help them practice their chant for the Healing. Nikki leads them in a practice and is gifted a prayer book as thanks.

Nikki and Momo investigate the notes written by the Saintess. They learn that the Saintess had woken up covered in wounds and missing her memories, and that she had investigated Docilism herself and discovered that it was twisting the faith of the true god Echeros. The Saintess had also found Echeros's sacred relics, Their "tears," being corrupted by Dark Essence. To slow the corruption and protect the "tears," the Saintess sealed them away with what remained of her strength. The Saintess also mentions that the Shroomling King had had a conflict with the Docilists.

Nikki and Momo meet up with Domibaba again and ask him about the Shroomling King, Abutata. Domibaba recalls that Abutata is usually gentle but that he had seen Abutata get extremely angry when the Docilists punished a Shroomling for singing the wrong words during a ceremony. Domibaba takes Nikki and Momo to the Shroomling Bathhouse to find Abutata.

At the Shroomling Bathhouse, they discover that Abutata and the items of several Shroomlings have gone missing. Nikki offers to investigate and eventually finds graffiti that looks like Abutata. Nikki manages to enter the wall and finds Abutata and the missing items. After rescuing Abutata, Nikki requests to talk to him privately and he invites them to the exclusive zone.

Synopsis — Part 3: The Pursuit of Truth
At the exclusive zone, Nikki and Momo discuss the Mothershroom's illness and their theory about Docilism's involvement with Abutata. Nikki and Momo decide to try looking for the Guardian Beast and Abutata suggests that they find a Shroomling named Urili who was the Guardian Beast's caretaker. Abutata asks Nikki for some styling tips when interacting with the Docilists.

At this time, the flowers for the Flower Offering Ceremony are ready. Nikki participates in the ceremony and Medael appears to talk with the Saintess alone. The two have a tense conversation and Nikki attempts to probe for Medael's true intentions, but Medael does not divulge much.

Nikki and Momo meet up with Domibaba at the Shroomling Market, who reports that Urili hasn't been to the market but that they may find him if they wait by the market entrance. By chance, they meet up with Wilhelm who is undercover as a fruit seller to investigate the Docilists. They share their findings with her.

At the market entrance, Nikki, Momo, and Domibaba meet with Urili. While Urili doesn't know of the Guardian Beast's current whereabouts, he does know that other snails have been acting up at night and believes that their behaviour is related to the Guardian Beast. Following this clue, Nikki, Momo, and Domibaba wait for nightfall and discover the snails are all heading in a certain direction.

Characters: (none listed on the wiki)

Synopsis — Part 1: Treasures and Secrets
After discovering the strange behavior of the snails, Nikki, Momo, and Abutata follow the snails to the Shiny Vault. The trio theorize about why the snails have converged on the vault and Nikki wonders if the missing Guardian Beast is actually being held inside the vault. Domibaba explains that the Docilists have taken over the vault and that no Shroomling has been allowed inside ever since. While looking for a way inside, they spot Abutata arguing with one of the Docilist guards and meet up with him.

Abutata explains that he had gone to ask for Shroomlings to be allowed inside the vault again. The Docilists had denied all entry applications, even if the paperwork had been done correctly. The trio explain their theory about the Guardian Beast's whereabouts and ask him if he knows another way into the vault. After looking around, Abutata invokes his luck and creates a hole in the side of the vault that Nikki can enter through.

Nikki and Momo enter the vault and discover that Docilism guards are watching over the vault. Carefully avoiding being spotted, they make their way to the exhibition hall on the top floor. There, they find the Guardian Beast's collar and Nikki experiences a vision when picking it up. In her vision, she sees Echeros, the Guardian Beast, and the collar. At that moment, the Guardian Beast's cries interrupt them and alert the Docilism guards that there are intruders. Nikki and Momo make a quick escape upwards and out of the vault.

Synopsis — Part 2: The Last Song of Battle
After escaping the Docilism guards in the Shiny Vault, Nikki and Momo reconvene with Domibaba and Wilhelm. Together, they hatch a plan to expose the Docilist plot. Domibaba and Wilhelm would attend the Healing of the Mothershroom and Wilhelm would disguise herself as the Saintess to take Nikki's place. Meanwhile, Nikki and Momo would head to the top of Spira's shell to find the Guardian Beast and bring it back to the Mothershroom. With their plan set, the group splits up.

Nikki and Momo ride Swanara to the top of Spira where they confront the corrupted Guardian Beast. After battling the Guardian Beast, Nikki seizes an opportunity to purify its body of Dark Essence and return the collar to it. She has a vision of herself, Momo, and Echeros witnessing the birth of the Mothershroom and charging the Guardian Beast, a snail named Whirls, with the Mothershroom's protection. Afterwards, she and Momo learn from the Guardian Beast that Medael had driven it away in order to target the Mothershroom.

With the Guardian Beast restored, they set off to return to stop Medael at the Healing ceremony. During the ceremony, Wilhelm is exposed as an imposter but Nikki and the Guardian Beast arrive in time to prevent anyone from being harmed. Nikki exposes Medael's plot to deceive the Shroomlings to seize the Mothershroom's power, but she and Domibaba are unable to keep Medael from escaping.

With Medael gone, the lies of Docilism begin to dissipate and the true god's remnants converge in the Heart of Infinity and make a Miracle Outfit, Providence Immemorial, reappear. Nikki uses the power of the Miracle Outfit to help the Guardian Beast truly heal the Mothershroom.

Characters: Abutata, Domibaba, Guardian Beast, Medael, Momo, Nikki, Wilhelm

ITZALAND CHAPTER 5: LIGHT ON OLD SOIL, A NEW CHAPTER BEGINS
===================================================================
Quest Type: Main Quest
Position: The fifteenth chapter in the Main Questline and the sixth chapter of the Itzaland arc.
Previous Chapter: Itzaland Chapter 4: Ancient Vows, Healing Rituals
Next Chapter: Itzaland Chapter 6: Land of the Slumbering Dragons

Quests:
- Where Desire and Goal Meet

Total Rewards:
- Mira EXP: 1K
- Diamond: 50
- Bling: 20K
- Thread of Purity: 120
- Shiny Bubbles: 100

Synopsis:
After resting at the home of the Saintess, Nikki and Momo receive a message from Abutata. They meet up with him and he explains that the Shroomlings will hold the Shroomling Gathering, an event that had been suspended for almost three years. He invites the two to the event. On the way to Hero's Square, he tells them that he has resolved to become a better king for the Shroomlings at Domibaba's encouragement.

At Hero's Square, Nikki and Momo catch up with Domibaba and Urili. Domibaba has been asked by Abutata to speak at the gathering, while Urili is encouraged to continue taking care of the snails.

When the gathering starts, Abutata thanks Nikki for her contributions to stopping Medael and Nikki unveils the truth about Docilism, the Mothershroom's illness, the Guardian Beast, and the true god Echeros. Domibaba gives a rousing speech that refocuses the Shroomlings' faith to Echeros's true identity instead of Docilism's claim that he was the Titan King. As the first step of moving on, Abutata declares that the Docilism church would be demolished and, at Nikki's suggestion, a Stylist's Guild would be built in its place.

The Shroomlings quickly set to work building the Stylist's Guild and invite Nikki to add the finishing brushstroke to the guild's flag. Nikki and Momo reunite with Wilhelm, who is ready to set off on the next part of her journey, and they speculate on Medael's true intentions and the missing Soul Spring water.

Returning to the home of the Saintess, Nikki and Momo reunite with the singing Shroomlings who ask her to tell them the story of Echeros. While telling the story, the Mothershroom transforms Nikki's outfit into Born Flawless.

Medael, having safely escaped, sails on a boat into foggy waters with the mechanical seed from the Mothershroom.

Characters: Abutata, Angert, Annatata, Bululu, Domibaba, Hilsetti, Medael, Meghill, Miyaya, Momo, Nikki, Urili, Wilhelm
