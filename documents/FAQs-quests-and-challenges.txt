DESCRIPTION: Help with Forced Perspective quests not working
CONTENT: Why aren't the Forced Perspective quests working?
You will need to get a gold box surrounding the target. If it's not showing up, please try resetting your camera to default settings and/or make sure aperture is set to f/16. Also ensure that "Hide Nikki" is ON if they are not required for the photo.

COMMAND: -astralswan
CATEGORY: Quests and Challenges
DESCRIPTION: Help with the Astral Swan quest
CONTENT: How do I complete the Astral Swan quest?
After completing the quest (Soaring Above the Starry Sky), groom the swan and follow these steps:
    Switch to the gliding outfit (Floral Memory)
    Use the ability near the swan to trigger a cutscene
        The button will glow in the correct spot.
    When the cutscene starts, DO NOT MOVE. If you move, the quest will not be completed and you will need to replay it.

COMMAND: -dailywishes
CATEGORY: Quests and Challenges
DESCRIPTION: Locations and tips for daily wish quests for Silver Petals
CONTENT: Where do I go for the daily Silver Petal wishes?
It should be given to you from a bottle in Daily Wishes (text will be random). If you do not have a bottle with this reward, you may need to progress further in the main quest or wait another day.
After you get 20 Silver Petals (enough to craft the outfit twice), you will no longer get Silver Petal bottles.
For photos, please remember only photos that are taken with the golden box outline on the NPC or target will count. Try zooming out more, hiding Nikki, or making sure your aperture is set to f/16!

COMMAND: -interlude
CATEGORY: Quests and Challenges
DESCRIPTION: How to access the Interlude chapter "Call of Beginnings"
CONTENT: How do I unlock the Interlude chapter Call of Beginnings?
Please make sure you have completed all 8 chapters of the main story and the following World quest, "Fifteen Years, Echoes of Wishes", by talking to Giroda in the Temple of Wishes (Grand Millewish Tree). Then unlock the Aurosa makeup node in the Heart of Infinity as shown here.

I can't begin the Interlude quest!
If you've met all the prerequisites for the Interlude and still can't start it, try completing the world quest Miss Detective! I want to become a Columnist!, located at "Barrel Home" in the Abandoned District. It unlocks after completing the exclusive quests Sin in the Suitcase and The Final Case.

A game restart may be required after completing the world quest for the Interlude to begin.
Please note that this is currently a bug. If it still doesn't start, please submit a bug report.

CONTENT: How do I get Sparkling Shell Crystals?

    Find any sick snails around (They are snails with 3 blue wiggly lines above their heads)

After doing the above, feed the snail with Snailfruit. You can feed the snail up to 3 times to get 3 Little Sparkling Shell Crystals.

COMMAND: -stoptrain
CATEGORY: Quests and Challenges
DESCRIPTION: How to stop the Choo Choo Train for the quest "Junior Conductor, Set Off!"
CONTENT: How do I stop the Choo-Choo Train?
You must stop the first red carriage within the golden box. If you overshoot, reverse and pause until the nose of the train is inside the box.

Note: To pass the test, you only need to disembark 1 passenger. You do not need to press continue and you may exit immediately.

COMMAND: -story
CATEGORY: Quests and Challenges
DESCRIPTION: How to unlock Itzaland and the prologue
CONTENT: How do I begin the new main story quests/go to Itzaland?
Note: There is no difference between these two methods, both will show the same cutscene immediately upon reaching Itzaland. The Sea of Stars is the faster method. Skipping Prologue is about new players skipping the 1.0 story.

Method 1: Sea of Stars
Go to Unbound Wharf and choose to start the new story, then you'll have a cutscene summarizing 1.0 before being teleported to the "Itzaland Canyon East" Warp Spire at the edge of Abandoned District.

Method 2: Abandoned District
Go to new gliding station at top of Windrider Mill (use Windrider Mill Top Warp Spire and walk north) and talk to the Gliding Station guy (Bright Eye Roundy) to activate the Skypass to the Itzaland Warp Spire.
After gliding, you'll have a cutscene summarizing 1.0, then unlock the "Itzaland Canyon East" Warp Spire.
