OVERVIEW
===================================================================
Outfits is a clothing category in Infinity Nikki. They are clothing sets comprised of wardrobe items.

Once a suit is completed, the user will receive a gift. Pieces of each outfit can be obtained in multiple ways.

Categories: Terminology, Wardrobe Categories, Released in Version 1.0.
