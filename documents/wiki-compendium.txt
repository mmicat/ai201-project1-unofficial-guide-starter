===================================================================
COMPENDIUM
===================================================================
Type: Gameplay

The Compendium shows all clothing items, outfit sets, Creatures, Lore, and Momo Cloaks currently in game. It can be accessed through the Pear-Pal and includes a search function.

===================================================================
RATING (COLLECTION POINTS)
===================================================================
Each clothing item is worth a different number of Collection Points when evaluated. Reaching different Collection Point milestones grants rewards including Titles, Cards, Diamonds, and Resonite Crystals.

- Every 100 Collection Points grants 1 star.
- Each "rank" consists of 10 stars.
- Each star awards Diamond ×30, with two exceptions:
  - The 5-star milestone awards Diamond ×30 and 1 Resonite Crystal.
  - The 10-star milestone awards Diamond ×100 and a corresponding Title (and later, a Card).

===================================================================
SUB-COMPENDIUMS
===================================================================
- Lore Compendium — shows all available Readables in game. (Main article: Readable)
- Cloak Compendium — shows all available Momo's Cloaks in game. (Main article: Momo's Cloak)
- Creature Compendium — shows all available Creatures in game. (Main article: Creature)
- Outfit Compendium — shows all available Clothing for Nikki in game.
